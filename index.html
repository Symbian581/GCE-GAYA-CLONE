About GCE Gaya

Gaya College of Engineering, Gaya is one of the premier technical institutions in Bihar, established in 1986. The college is affiliated to Aryabhatta Knowledge University, Patna and approved by AICTE, New Delhi.

The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines with state-of-the-art infrastructure and highly qualified faculty members.
